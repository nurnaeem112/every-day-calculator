Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is a Crypto Profit Calculator accurate?

Yes, it provides accurate results based on the data you enter. However, accuracy depends on including all details such as buy price, sell price, and transaction fees.

Do I need to include fees in my calculation?

Including fees ensures more precise profit or loss results. Many exchanges charge trading fees, which can affect your final earnings.

Can I calculate unrealized profits?

Yes, by entering your purchase price and the current market price, you can estimate unrealized gains or losses on your holdings.

Which cryptocurrencies can I use this calculator for?

You can use it for any cryptocurrency, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Dogecoin, and many others, as long as you know the buy and sell prices.

Why should I track my crypto profits?

Tracking profits helps you manage investments better, identify successful trading strategies, and prepare for tax reporting requirements.
