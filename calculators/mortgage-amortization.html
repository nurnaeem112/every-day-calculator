What is a Mortgage?

A mortgage is a loan from a bank or lender that helps you buy a home. Instead of paying the full price upfront, you borrow the money and agree to pay it back in monthly installments. Your house itself acts as collateral, meaning the lender can take it back if you fail to make payments.
