Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure that uses your weight and height to estimate whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

How is BMI calculated?

BMI is calculated differently for US (imperial) units and metric units. For US units, it uses pounds and inches, and for metric units, it uses kilograms and meters.

What do the BMI numbers mean?

A BMI below 18.5 is considered underweight, 18.5â€“24.9 is normal, 25â€“29.9 is overweight, and 30 or above is obese. These categories help assess health risks.

Can I use this calculator with both US and Metric units?

Yes, the BMI Calculator supports both systems. You can enter your weight and height in pounds and inches (US) or kilograms and meters (Metric).

Is BMI a perfect measure of health?

BMI is a useful screening tool but does not account for muscle mass, bone density, or fat distribution. Always consult a healthcare professional for a comprehensive assessment.

Why should I use a BMI Calculator?

Using a BMI Calculator helps you quickly assess your body weight category, monitor changes over time, and support health and fitness planning.
